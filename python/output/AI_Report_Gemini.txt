*   **Link to Review Scores**: As identified in product performance, the **negative correlation between `Review Score` and `Delivery Days`** underscores the importance of efficient delivery in maintaining overall customer satisfaction. Longer delivery times not only affect the perception of service but also impact how customers rate the products received.

## 4. Conclusions & Actionable Recommendations

### 4.1 Overview of Main Insights and Conclusions

The e-commerce marketplace exhibits strong revenue growth and effective execution of promotional events, particularly around November. However, significant challenges exist in customer retention, with exceptionally low repeat purchase rates indicating a reliance on new customer acquisition. Operational efficiency, especially concerning delivery times and seller service quality, directly impacts customer satisfaction as reflected in product and seller review scores. Furthermore, there's a heavy geographic and category concentration of revenue, posing potential risks if these core areas face disruption. The disparity between top revenue-generating provinces and those with high average customer value points to untapped market potential.
